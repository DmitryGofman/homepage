About

I am a mechanical engineer with 6+ years of hands-on R&D and systems integration experience. My background spans mechanical design, electromechanical systems, prototyping, manufacturing, and product development. I combine technical rigor with direct making, exploring how design connects to awareness, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

I work at the intersection of mechanical engineering, hands-on prototyping, system integration, and AI-assisted workflows. I practice classical Hatha Yoga and believe in maintaining precision of body and mind as a foundation for building systems that matter.

My direction is toward hardware startups, practical design, elegant systems, and multidisciplinary thinking — building real things that serve real needs.

How I Think

Core principles guiding my work and practice.

  • Build with precision.

    Detail matters. Systems work when every part fits.

  • Learn across disciplines.

    The best solutions come from connecting ideas across fields.

  • Keep the body and mind sharp.

    Physical practice is not separate from intellectual work.

  • Use technology without losing awareness.

    Tools should enhance, not replace, direct experience.

  • Turn ideas into working systems.

    Thinking without making is incomplete.

  • Prefer direct experience over theory alone.

    Understanding comes through doing.
